Layout #101

  • Components:  Top App Bar
  • Behavior: Scrolling upward hides the top app bar, scrolling downward reveals the top app bar, thus maximizing space available for content on smaller phone screens.
  • Recommendations:
    1. Make sure there is enough space in Top App Bar for icons and title on smaller phone screens.
    2. If a title in Top App Bar is too long, switch to short version on phone breakpoint.
